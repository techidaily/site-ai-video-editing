How Long Does It Take To Edit Videos

If you want to put together the pieces for online videos the number one thing most of the time takes is editing.

Why does editing take so much time and what to do for that?

  • Too many choices.

It completely depends upon how many choices you are giving yourself while editing a video. It takes time to make choices. For example, if your friend asks you to buy a card (any card) from the store. It depends upon how much footage you have on your edit and what kind of content you are editing. Some of the content is really complex and takes a lot of time like V-logging but at the same time, you may have content that requires very less edit or zero edit.

Part 1. What Is MP4 Video Format?

MP4 file format is mostly used for video files. The original data is not in MP4 format. The format can also contain audio, video, subtitles, or image data in different streams. All the streams can be decoded by different decoders.

MP4 video format is developed by the Moving Picture Expert Group (MPEG). MP4 file format is compatible with a wide range of software and devices. It has relatively high post-compressed video quality thus takes less space than other formats.
