How to Clone Yourself in Videos with Filmora?

With the help of video editing programs like Filmora , you can create clones of yourself. Whether youre looking to create a funny skit or a serious video, cloning yourself can add much production value to your project.

In this article, well walk you through the process of how to clone yourself in videos using Filmora.

Part 1: How to Film Clips for Cloning Effects?

To make cloning effects, you will need to take two different shots and use a tripod to place the camera, so the frame doesnt change.

Shooting the first clip

For the first shot, stay on one side of the frame and do the first action. For example, you can pretend to yank and take off your jacket. Then, drop the jacket to the other side of the frame.

To make it more realistic, make an interaction by reacting to the clone while facing the other direction.
