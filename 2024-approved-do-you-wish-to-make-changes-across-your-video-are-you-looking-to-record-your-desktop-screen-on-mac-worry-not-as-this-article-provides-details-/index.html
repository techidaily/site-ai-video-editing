Step 1: You need to open the appropriate video across QuickTime to convert it accordingly. Once opened, tap on the â€œFileâ€ tab, and select â€œExport asâ€ across the available options.

Step 2: You need to select an appropriate resolution from the available options according to the video quality that you want the video to be saved in on the preceding menu. QuickTime player will show low-quality resolutions for the high-quality video but not vice versa.

Step 3: A new window opens that requires you to set up the name and location for the video. Add the file extension â€œ.movâ€ at the end of the name of the video to convert it into MOV format.
